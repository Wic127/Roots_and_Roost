About the Vision

At Roots and Roost, we blend modern architecture, off-grid sustainability, and the simple pleasures of Caribbean island life. Our container homes are solar-powered, surrounded by lush fruit trees, and complete with outdoor pools, chickens for fresh eggs, and locally rooted experiences.

Each property offers a unique escape where modern comfort meets natural harmony, creating unforgettable memories in paradise.
